Target speed

You need to get the ship's length from the recognition manual!


Make sure your u-boat has stopped. Then put your periscope's vertical line in front of the target ship and start your stopwatch by pressing T by default. Stop your stopwatch when the target ship has passed the periscope's vertical line and input the data below.

Ship length (m)
Stopwatch time (s)
Target speed (kt)


Target range

You need to get the ship's mast height from the recognition manual!


Put your periscope's horizontal line on the target ship's waterline and count how many centiradians (horizontal lines) it takes to reach the highest point of the highest mast of the target ship.
You can use decimals.

Mast height (m)
Centiradians
Range (hm)
